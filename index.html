About Juniper International

Established in 2015, Juniper International Pvt. Ltd. is a legally registered private limited company in Nepal, operating as an importer, distributor, and supplier of medical, surgical, and healthcare products across the country.

We focus on supplying essential medical consumables, surgical products, and hospital equipment to healthcare institutions. Our company works with international manufacturers and suppliers to ensure that high-quality, safe, and reliable healthcare products are available in the Nepalese market.

Registered under the Companies Act, 2006, and registered with the Office of the Company Registrar, Ministry of Industry, Government of Nepal, we maintain the highest standards of quality and service.
