About Juniper International

Leading medical equipment distribution in Nepal since 2015

Company Overview

Juniper International Pvt. Ltd. is a legally registered private limited company in Nepal, incorporated on 23 July 2015 under the Companies Act, 2006.

We operate as an importer, distributor, and supplier of medical, surgical, and healthcare products across Nepal. Our company focuses on supplying essential medical consumables, surgical products, and hospital equipment to healthcare institutions.

We work with international manufacturers and suppliers to ensure that high-quality, safe, and reliable healthcare products are available in the Nepalese market. Our distribution network covers all regions of Nepal, ensuring timely delivery to hospitals, clinics, and healthcare centers.

Our Journey

2015

Company incorporation under the Companies Act, 2006, Government of Nepal. Started operations with basic medical consumables.

2016

Expanded product range to include surgical products and established partnerships with 3 international manufacturers.

2017

Extended distribution network to cover Kathmandu Valley and major cities across Nepal. Added infusion equipment to product portfolio.

2018

Established modern warehousing facility with temperature-controlled storage for medical products.

2019

Partnered with 10+ international healthcare manufacturers. Reached 200+ healthcare institution clients.

2020

Played crucial role in supplying PPE and medical equipment during COVID-19 pandemic. Expanded to all 77 districts of Nepal.

2023

Current: Serving 500+ healthcare partners with 1000+ medical products. Continuing to expand product range and services.
